## Party Finder Terms

### Toxic Friends

Raidplan for phase 1.

### Roles Mortal Slayer

Specific Mortal Slayer Strategy with its own raidplan seperate of Toxic Friends

### Modified Bin

Slightly modified bin for 12S Strategies, usually means they are using the entire pastebin. 3VJ0 are the last couple letters of the url.

### Uptime

Refers to Uptime Idyllic.

### Banana Codex

Specific Rep2 Strat not included in the main pastebins, "relative D north".
