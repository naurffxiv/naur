## Party Finder Terms

### Hector

Video guide created by Hector.

### TF

Refers to the Toxic Friends (TF) Raidplan commonly used.

### Kindred No Buddies:

Kindred Flatliner Strategy.

### Toxic No Buddies:

Toxic Friends Flatliner Strategy.

### No Buddies:

"buddies" was an early strategy where the prey players pair up with tethers and mirror their tower knockbacks for
Flatliner, **No buddies is not doing that.**

### PTT or TTT or PPP etc:

Order of tower soaks at the end of the fight, P is party, T is tank. I.e. PTT = Party, Tank, Tank.

### Uptime or Fixed ES:

Two strategies for Ecliptic Stampede - the two fold fire or four fold fire mechanic. Raidplans included below.

## Arena Markers

### Note about Flatliner:

Both Toxic Friends and Kindred use **nearly identical flatliner** strategies and their only difference comes down to
where you stand on a static marker. Please be wary about whether or not your marker set has prey standing on inside
corner or outside corner of the marker. Ask your PF Lead which they placed!
